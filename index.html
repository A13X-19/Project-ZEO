Welcome to Project ZEO

Project ZEO is the next step towards high-grade, advanced drone technology. Funded by the International Conglomerate Reticence, ZEO is the next step towards global security and international peace between foreign powers.

What is Zeo?

Project ZEO is the development of a highly advanced aerial fleet consisting of a series of large and small mobile aircraft capable of transporting public, government and military cargo over long-distance areas. With the development of this air fleet, codenamed Revenant, another series of aircraft designed specifically for the deployment militargy-grade aerial drone from low-orbit is also underway. These defense craft, unofficially designated as the ZEO-Series, are designed with the goal of ensuring the well-being of not just the Revenant Fleet but also any and all allied nations across the planet.

Current production cycle and development is underway, presumably to be completed around the year 2030 given the rate Reticence's production facilities preform on a daily (this also included the Conglomerate's main Branch and city-HQ, Subsidia). Because of this, only two know aircraft have been fully developed and are in the process of test flight and navigation-combat scenarios. They are designated ZEO-Bird 1 and Arsenal Zen 1; currently they are the only two official ZEO-Series units, as Reticence itself plans to postpone ZEO construction till the halfway mark of the Revenant Fleet's profuction has been completed.
