Welcome to Project ZEO

Project ZEO is the next step towards high-grade, advanced drone technology. Funded by the Internation Conglomerate Reticence, ZEO is the next step towards international peace and security.

What is Zeo?

Project ZEO is the creation of a highly advanced aerial drone aircraft capable of transporting a variety of smaller drones; as a result, these low-orbiting drone carriers - unofficially designated as the ZEO-series - are designed with the goal of providing any form of public, government or military support to any country on the planet.

The current rate of development cycling and building progress counts around the two main class variants of the ZEO Project, with room for futher development of potential varitations in the far future; these craft types will designated ZEO-Bird and Arsenal ZEO.
